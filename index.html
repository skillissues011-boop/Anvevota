Theoretical Foundation

The Self-Priming Mechanism.

Our project is built upon the foundational principles of Archimedes and Nikola Tesla, heavily advanced by the mechanical studies conducted by James Watt and his contemporary, James Pickard.

The Antevorta Turbine operates on our proprietary Self-Priming Load Method. Initiated via Triggering Technology, the system requires absolutely no external resources or combustible fuel to commence operations.

Once triggered, the turbine generates internal electrical power within the first 60 seconds. This electricity is used to precisely position the Load Piston at a calculated location, locking the system into continuous, 24/7 motion. To maintain this perpetual operation, the turbine uses a maximum of only 20% of its self-generated electrical power, leaving the vast majority available for grid export.
