Solving the Duck Curve

Mastering Grid Stability.

The global transition to variable renewable energy (VRE) suffers from severe intermittency. Solar and wind generation creates massive fluctuations in grid frequency, requiring expensive peaker plants to bridge the "Duck Curve" generation gap.

Antevorta solves the baseline power dilemma. Our prototype is engineered to provide continuous, dispatchable power with integrated reactive power support. We eliminate the need for massive, ecologically disruptive battery energy storage systems (BESS).

Core Infrastructure Architecture

Redefining Generation

A novel thermodynamic conversion process engineered for heavy industrial load centers, hyperscale data centers, and national grid modernization.

High-Density Synthetic Inertia

Provides critical grid stability lacking in traditional inverter-based renewables. Our core natively supplies reactive power and frequency regulation, mimicking the inertial response of heavy synchronous generators.

Microgrid & Islanding Ready

Integrates seamlessly with IEEE 1547 standards for distributed energy resources. Offers grid-forming capabilities and black-start functionality to ensure critical infrastructure never experiences downtime.

HVDC Transmission Optimized

Co-locating generation near load centers reduces high-voltage transmission step-down losses by up to 14%. Direct high-voltage direct current (HVDC) out-coupling available for macro-grid injection.
